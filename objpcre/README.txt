To install/utilize ObjPCRE simple include: 'pcre.h', 'objpcre.h', 'objpcre.m' and 'libpcre.a' in your project and build. Thats it!

Included in this zip: 'pcre.h', 'objpcre.h', 'objpcre.m'
Build your own pcre lib or grab it from: http://sourceforge.net/projects/objpcre/
